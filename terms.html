What we do

UrClaw installs custom AI workflows on client-owned hardware. Engagements run through three or four phases (discovery, scope, install, optional retainer) at one of three tiers (Starter Install, Operations Install, Full Operations Build). The website describes the practice. The binding scope of any engagement is the signed engagement memorandum, which controls if it conflicts with anything on this page.
